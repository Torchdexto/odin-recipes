Garlic Bread Recipe

A delicious loaf of garlic bread, toasted to perfection.

Looking for a fast and easy way to make what is arguably the best type of bread? Look no further!

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette
  • 1 stick of butter, softened
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 2 tbsp. freshly chopped parsley
  • 1/4 grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 and line large baking sheet with tin foil.
  2. In a small bowl, mix softened butter with garlic, salt, and parsley until well combined.
  3. Spread cut sides of bread liberally with garlic butter. Sprinkle with Parmesan.
  4. Bake until bread is toasty and golden at the edges, about 10 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
